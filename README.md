# 女友（Girlfriends）

[English](#english) | [中文](#中文)

---

## English

**Girlfriends** is a family-friendly companion-raising mod for Minecraft survival mode. Explore the world, meet five distinct girls, build bonds through gifts and quests, and let them become your long-term partners — enhancing your survival, building, and combat journey without ever playing the game for you.

### Five Unique Characters

Each girl has her own personality, backstory, and gameplay niche, ensuring every relationship feels fresh:

| Character | Theme |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Momo** (沫沫) | Nature, flowers, and gardening |
| **Yuxi** (渔溪) | Ocean, fishing, and sailing |
| **Meishu** (梅疏) | Mining, crafting, and industry |
| **Wanying** (晚萤) | Combat, protection, and courage |
| **Youruo** (幽若) | Exploration, mystery, and discovery |

### Core Gameplay Loop

**Build Affection** — Give gifts, complete quests, and spend time together. Affection grows from stranger to intimate companion, with each stage unlocking new interactions and dialogue.

**Quest System** — Each girl offers a fixed questline telling her personal story (~15–25 hours per character), plus randomized repeatable quests for ongoing engagement. Quest slots are unique per character — you can only pursue one quest per girl at a time.

**Follow & Blessing** — Once your bond is strong enough, a girl can follow you through the world, granting unique blessing buffs tied to her theme. MoMo enhances your green thumb, Yuxi calms the seas, MeiShu reveals the riches underground — each blessing makes a different part of vanilla Minecraft more rewarding.

**Home Together** — Invite your companion to share a home. She will heal you, gain passive affection daily, and fill your base with life. But living together isn't always peaceful — occasional squabbles keep things real.

**Multiplayer Competition** — On servers, multiple players can pursue the same girl. A binding system with a 3-day shake-up period adds social tension: will your rival steal her heart, or will your bond hold firm?

### Design Philosophy

- **Enhance, don't replace** — Characters make vanilla gameplay more meaningful, not automated
- **Feel the bond, don't crunch numbers** — Fuzzy progress bars and behavioral feedback, not spreadsheets
- **Light narrative, heavy atmosphere** — Short task cards and environmental changes tell the story, not walls of text
- **All-ages wholesome** — The focus is companionship, promises, and shared life; no adult content

### Requirements

- Minecraft Java Edition 26.1+
- NeoForge 26.1.2+

## 中文

**《女友》（Girlfriends）** 是一款面向 Minecraft 生存模式的全年龄功能性美少女养成模组。在探索世界时自然地遇见五位各具主题的少女，通过赠礼、委托与陪伴逐步建立羁绊，让她们成为你在生存、建设与战斗旅程中的长期伙伴——她们增强你的体验，却从不替你玩游戏。

### 五位各具主题的角色

每位少女都拥有独立的性格、故事与玩法侧重，让每一段关系都独一无二：

| 角色 | 主题 |
| :-- | :-- |
| **沫沫** | 自然、花卉与园艺 |
| **渔溪** | 海洋、垂钓与航海 |
| **梅疏** | 采矿、锻造与工业 |
| **晚萤** | 战斗、守护与勇气 |
| **幽若** | 探索、谜境与发现 |

### 核心玩法闭环

**好感养成** — 赠礼、完成委托、日常陪伴，好感度从陌生逐步升温至亲密伴侣。每个关系阶段都会解锁新的互动方式与角色台词。

**委托系统** — 每位角色拥有一条专属固定委托线，讲述她的个人故事（单角色约 15~25 小时），并辅以随机刷新且带过期时限的日常委托，让后期互动仍有目标。每角色仅有一个委托槽，固定委托优先于随机委托——你需要用心规划与她的每一步。

**跟随与祝福** — 当羁绊足够深厚，她将愿意跟随你走遍世界，并提供与其主题相关的祝福增益。沫沫助你绿意盎然，渔溪令你扬帆破浪，梅疏为你揭示矿藏——每种祝福都让原版 Minecraft 的某一维度更加值得投入。

**家园同居** — 邀请她成为家园伙伴，共享一张双人床。她会为你回血、每日自然增长好感、让基地充满生活气息。但同居不总是风平浪静——偶尔的小争执让羁绊更显真实。

**多人轻竞争** — 在服务器中，多位玩家可竞争同一位角色的青睐。绑定机制与 3 天动摇期制造微妙的社交张力：你的情敌会夺走她的心，还是你们之间的羁绊牢不可破？

### 设计理念

- **陪伴增强，而非替代** — 角色让原版玩法更有温度和动力，而非自动化的代劳工具
- **关系可感知，而非可计算** — 模糊进度条与行为反馈呈现羁绊成长，拒绝冰冷的数值驱动
- **轻叙事，重氛围** — 短句任务卡与环境变化推进剧情，拒绝大段文本打断游玩节奏
- **全年龄向，纯爱羁绊** — 核心是陪伴、约定与共同生活，不涉及成人内容

### 环境要求

- Minecraft Java Edition 26.1+
- NeoForge 26.1.2+
